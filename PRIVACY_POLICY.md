# Privacy Policy for FocusBox


I am committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application, FocusBox.

## 1. Information Collection and Use

### Notification Data
FocusBox is designed to help you manage distractions by "boxing" notifications. To provide this service, the app requires access to your notifications via the Android Notification Listener Service.
- **Local Processing**: All notification content (app name, title, and body) is processed and stored **locally** on your device in a private database.
- **No Cloud Uploads**: We do **not** upload your notification data to any servers. Your private messages and alerts stay on your phone.

### Usage Statistics
The app calculates statistics such as "Distractions Avoided" and "Saved Time". This data is generated locally and is not shared with us or any third party.

## 2. Third-Party Services
The app uses the following third-party services which may collect information used to identify you:

- **Google Play Services**: Used for core app functionality and app updates.
- **Google AdMob**: Used to display advertisements. AdMob may collect device identifiers and usage data to serve personalized or non-personalized ads.
- **Google Play Billing**: Used to process "Pro Version" purchases. Your payment information is handled securely by Google and is never shared with us.

## 3. Data Storage and Deletion
- All app data (schedules, muted apps, and boxed notifications) is stored in the app's private storage on your device.
- You can clear your "Notification Box" or delete specific schedules at any time within the app.
- Uninstalling the app will delete all locally stored data associated with FocusBox.

## 4. Children's Privacy
FocusBox is not intended for children under the age of 13 (or 16 in certain jurisdictions). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we discover that a child has provided us with personal information, we will delete it immediately.

## 5. Permissions
The app requests the following sensitive permissions:
- **Notification Access**: Required to intercept and manage your incoming notifications.
- **Post Notifications**: Required to deliver your "boxed" notifications at your scheduled times.

## 6. Changes to This Policy
We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. You are advised to review this page periodically for any changes.
